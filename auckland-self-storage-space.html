TIPS FOR USING SELF STORAGE UNITS IN AUCKLAND

People have been using storage units for over the years, and everyone has his or her reason for occupying a paid storage space. But to keep your belongings secure we have come up with a few tips of utilizing the storage room which is immensely talented.

1. Choose a reliable Self Storage unit provider

When you pay for a storage room to secure your belongings, you are relying on a third party by putting in your test. Everything that you decide to keep instead of disposing of has some value to you. Thus, it is essential to find a reliable company even if your belongings are not much expensive. Try to review the online feedbacks of the company or ask your peers and relatives who have experience in a storage room. Don’t look for a cheap storage room which is offering less competitive rates as they might not be able to provide security to your possessions. Search for a famous and reliable storage unit provider near your residence or business.

2. Take time to research

Don’t just rust at the last minute to find a storage unit provider as there are several essential aspects linked as the convenience, the cost and most importantly the size of the storage unit. Thus, make sure you have checked all the three requirements start researching before it’s too late. Proper research will help you trace the best providers in your area. It is always better to do appropriate, timely research rather than running late a creating a hassle.

3. Note down your inventory before and after you rent a storing unit

Whys is it essential to maintain a record of your stored or to be stored items? There are two important reasons:

  • You will be sure about the size of the storage space you need
  • Things will be organized and secured

You don’t need to be extra conscious to maintain a proper record, but it is essential to have a general record to claim in times of any mishap. Moreover, you will get an idea of the storage unit size, and you will only have to pay for the occupied space, not for extra space.

4. Label your boxes before storing

Renting a storage unit mean that you don’t need those items for a couple of months or years now. Do you think it will be easy for you to remember what you stored in which carton after months or years? I assure that your answer would be NO!!! It is better to put labels on each package mentioning the items stored in each box. Once your boxes are sealed, it would be a complete mess to unpack every box each time to search for a single belonging. The best solution to avoid this hassle is that you put a label on each carton so that you can quickly know which item is packed in which package.

5. Pack your things safe and protected

Even with the most expensive storage company claiming to provide the best environmental conditions, extra security and all checks you need to take preventive measures for your stored belongings. Time to time check and proper maintenance would be required. For instance, the wooden furniture should be wrapped in plastics or blankets as they can be damaged due to damp weather conditions. Have you stored your clothes in the storage unit? Don't forget to put in some anti-insect medicines inside the cartons so that rats or any other insect will not destroy your clothes. If you have stored some expensive, fragile items, they need to be adequately protected to avoid any damage while moving them to the storage unit and then back when needed. Thus, either it’s an expensive or inexpensive item one should be sure to provide extra protection to his or her belongings.

Whenever you have planned to store, be sure that you save smartly. The tips mentioned above will help you secure your belongings for a lifetime without any damage. To have the best storage experience one should keep the tips mentioned above in mind as storage can be a complete mess if not properly taken care off.
